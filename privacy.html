Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

Kuyili (“we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Kuyili mobile application and related services.

---

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information required to deliver voice messages as phone calls.

  • Mobile number provided by the user (sender)
  • Recipient mobile number (India)
  • User-recorded voice message
  • Device information (basic diagnostics)
  • App usage data for service improvement

We do not collect contacts, photos, location, or unrelated personal data.

---

2. Voice Data Handling

  • Voice messages are recorded only when the user initiates recording
  • Voice messages are used solely to deliver the call to the selected recipient
  • Voice data is stored temporarily and automatically deleted after delivery or expiry
  • We do not modify, generate, or impersonate voices
---

3. How We Use Information

Collected data is used strictly for:

  • Placing voice calls as requested by the user
  • Ensuring service reliability and delivery
  • Preventing misuse, spam, or fraudulent activity
  • Legal and regulatory compliance
---

4. Anti-Spam & Abuse Prevention

Kuyili enforces strict policies to prevent misuse.

  • No automated, or unsolicited calls
  • No impersonation or misleading messages
  • No political or marketing usage without approval
  • Accounts violating policies may be suspended or permanently banned
---

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. Data may be shared only with:

  • Telecom providers for call delivery
  • Legal authorities if required by law
---

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

---

7. User Responsibilities

  • You confirm that you have the right to contact the recipient
  • You agree not to misuse the service
  • You are responsible for the content of your voice message
---

8. Children’s Privacy

Kuyili is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

---

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
